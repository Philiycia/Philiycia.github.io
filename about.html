Freshwater Fish vs Saltwater Fish

Water Salinity

Freshwater: Less than 0.5 ppt (parts per thousand)

Saltwater: 30â€“40 ppt with dissolved minerals

Osmoregulation

Freshwater Fish: Absorb water naturally and must remove excess water through urine.

Saltwater Fish: Lose water naturally and must drink seawater while excreting excess salt.

Tank Maintenance

Freshwater: Easier and beginner-friendly

Saltwater: Requires precise salinity and advanced care

Cost

Freshwater: Lower equipment and livestock cost

Saltwater: Higher investment and specialized systems
