Popular Saltwater Aquarium Fish

Explore colorful marine species for reef and saltwater aquariums.

Clownfish

Scientific Name: Amphiprion ocellaris

Care Level: Beginner

Tank Size: Minimum 20 gallons

Clownfish are hardy and peaceful marine fish famous for their symbiotic relationship with sea anemones.

Blue Tang

Scientific Name: Paracanthurus hepatus

Care Level: Intermediate

Tank Size: Minimum 75 gallons

Blue Tangs are active swimmers requiring larger tanks and stable marine water conditions.

Lionfish

Scientific Name: Pterois volitans

Care Level: Advanced

Tank Size: Minimum 55 gallons

Lionfish are striking predators with venomous spines and require experienced marine aquarists.

Mandarinfish

Scientific Name: Synchiropus splendidus

Care Level: Advanced

Tank Size: Minimum 30 gallons

Mandarinfish are beautifully patterned but require mature reef tanks with live copepods.

Yellow Tang

Scientific Name: Zebrasoma flavescens

Care Level: Intermediate

Tank Size: Minimum 75 gallons

Yellow Tangs are algae grazers that help maintain reef tank cleanliness.

Royal Gramma

Scientific Name: Gramma loreto

Care Level: Beginner

Tank Size: Minimum 30 gallons

Royal Grammas are peaceful reef fish with vibrant purple and yellow coloration.
